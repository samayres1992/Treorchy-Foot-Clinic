Biography

Debra Ayres is a Fully Qualified Podiatrist holding a Bachelor of Science with honours (BSc hons) degree, and has been a member of the College of Podiatry for over 10 years. A Podiatrist is a protected title with the hcpc therefore ensuring the practice is fully insured and regulated. In 2012 Debra opened Treorchy Foot Clinic, and can be found on the high street.
